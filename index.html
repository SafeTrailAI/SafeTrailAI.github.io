How SafeTrail AI Works

A three-step pipeline: a Planner Agent drafts your route, then a Critic Agent verifies every detail against live safety data before you see it.

STEP 01
User Inputs

Enter your target mountain, planned date, experience level, and gear. SafeTrail collects everything needed to personalise your plan.

STEP 02
Planner Creates

The Generator Agent drafts a complete hiking plan — route, schedule, gear checklist, and difficulty rating — tuned to your profile.

STEP 03
Critic Verifies via RAG

The Critic Agent cross-checks against live weather, trail alerts, and permit data using RAG — revising anything unsafe before you see the result.

Why It Matters

Mountain Safety in Taiwan

Taiwan is home to over 268 peaks above 3,000 m, including Jade Mountain — the highest point in Northeast Asia at 3,952 m. Every year, dozens of hikers face emergencies due to sudden weather shifts, inadequate permits, and overestimated fitness levels. Taiwan's mountain weather can change from clear skies to dangerous conditions within hours, making real-time safety verification critical for every outing.

SafeTrail AI addresses this by combining a Generator Agent for intelligent route planning with a Critic Agent powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) — cross-referencing live data from the Central Weather Administration, National Park permit systems, and Search & Rescue incident records to ensure every plan it delivers is safe, legal, and personalised.
